WHAT DO I DO?

I am a creative, experienced and dynamic developer. I love programming anything and making unique, challenging and fun projects.

The field of Computer Science has been my passion since I first delved into it when I was 12. Since then I have strived to expand my skils and work my way towards my dream career. I currently study Computer Science at Cardiff University, I am in my final year and expect to graduate in June 2021. When I'm not doing uni work, I develop mobile apps on the side. I contacted a large online guitar tuition company, GuitarLessons365 and outlined my proposal for an application to accompany their courses. They agreed and since then I have been working with them and have created two high quality apps. The first was a web app which was integrated into their GL365 Academy section of their website. In 2020, I discontinued the web app in favor of an improved and more accessible mobile application for Android and iOS. This was a huge entrepreneurial achievement for me as the company has over 1 million YouTube subscribers, so this is huge exposure for my work. When I'm not programming, I spend time with my beautiful dog, Milo. I love clearing my head by taking him for long walks. I also play the guitar and attemt to write songs. One of my other passions is video editing. Every so often, I make a programming tutorial on my YouTube channel. It is another way for me to have fun, challenge myself and be creative.
